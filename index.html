# Welcome

My name is Daniel Filonik and I design and implement interaction environments for immersive data exploration and collaboration. This website documents my current personal projects and catalogues some of my previous work.

My work utilizes a wide range of technologies, like interactive screens, projections, augmented and virtual reality headsets, in order to create environments that enable people to become immersed in data. I have worked on a variety of applications ranging from expert systems for researchers and data scientists to interactive exhibits that facilitate science communication with general audiences.

I provide UI/UX design consulting services. Would you like to know more, or do you have a project to share with me? Please do not hesitate to get in touch.
